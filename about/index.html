About Explore Lori

An independent editorial platform for Lori Province, Northern Armenia — built to last as long as the monasteries.

An independent eye on the north

Lori Province is the least-documented corner of the South Caucasus. Twelve circuits of gorge, plateau, and forest hold medieval monasteries, Soviet-era copper towns, and villages where the 9th century and the 21st century exist in the same afternoon. Almost none of it has been written about with the care it deserves.

Explore Lori was founded on a single premise: that a place this complex deserves long-form attention. Not listicles. Not hotel recommendations. Long reads, photography essays, field notes, and reportage — written by people who have spent enough time in the gorge to notice when the light changes direction at noon.

Everything published here is original, independently reported, and seasonally timed. We do not accept press trips, sponsored content, or destination marketing. The work is editorially independent and always will be.
