Get involved

Connect with Explore Lori

All messages are read by a real person. We respond within 48 hours.

Missing from the map?

If you know a trail, ruin, viewpoint, spring, or village experience that isn't on the site — tell us about it. We review every submission within 2 weeks.

Know Lori deeply?

Trail guides, cultural walkers, craft workshop facilitators, village hosts. Free to list, no commission. We'll publish your profile within one week.

Have a place to stay?

From a spare room in a village house to a fully equipped guesthouse. Free listing — we'll be in touch before publishing.

Not sure where to start?

Tell us roughly what you're after and we'll point you in the right direction. No itineraries sold — just honest local advice. We respond within 48 hours.

Your information is only used to respond to your message.
