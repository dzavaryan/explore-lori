Lori vs Yerevan: Prices here are 30–50% lower than the capital. A 1-bedroom apartment in central Yerevan runs 255,000 AMD/month — in Vanadzor or a Lori village you can rent a whole house for 60,000–80,000 AMD. Groceries are similar, but eating out is noticeably cheaper.

All prices are estimates based on publicly available data and may vary by season. Summer (June–August) sees higher accommodation rates in popular spots like Haghpat.
