Lori vs Yerevan: Prices here are 30–50% lower than the capital. A 1-bedroom apartment in central Yerevan runs 255,000 AMD/month — in Vanadzor or a Lori village you can rent a whole house for 60,000–80,000 AMD.

All prices are estimates based on publicly available data. Summer (June–August) sees higher accommodation rates near Haghpat and Sanahin. 1 EUR ≈ 420 AMD (March 2026).
